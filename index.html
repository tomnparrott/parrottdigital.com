About Me

Hello, I'm Tom Parrott (Yes, the thing with all the parrot's is because of my name!) With years of hands-on coding experience, I've cultivated a robust foundation in web development long before obtaining my Computer Science degree from the University of London. My specialism in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning has further refined my ability to create innovative digital solutions.

My professional journey has taken me through a diverse range of projects, each teaching me something new. A highlight was my collaboration with TutorBloom in the EdTech sector. It was a venture that combined my love for web development with advanced functionalities, making learning more accessible and personalised for students everywhere.

What sets me apart? It's not just my extensive experience, but also my commitment to delivering quality without breaking the bank. I understand the importance of affordability in today's digital landscape, and I've always aimed to offer top-tier services at a cost-effective rate.

With a penchant for understanding client needs and translating them into tangible web solutions, I'm here to help. Whether you're looking for a bespoke website or optimising an existing platform, my expertise ensures a job well done, on time and within budget. Let's turn your digital aspirations into reality.
